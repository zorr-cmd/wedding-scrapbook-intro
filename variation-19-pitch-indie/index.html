director's statement

i don't really know what this is yet. i started filming the boys in 2017 because i had a camera and they were funny. i thought it was a documentary. now i think it's something else.

we've been to egypt. we've been to the bottom of a canyon. we've been to times square at 3am. we've been to a mountain at sunset where the light got so good none of us spoke for ten minutes.

i don't know what genre this is. it's a documentary. it's not a documentary. it's not a fiction. it's not a home movie. it's about eight men who keep showing up and a camera that keeps watching.

i'm pitching this to you now because [GROOM] is getting married and the film needs an ending. or maybe a new beginning. i'll know when we shoot it.

thank you for reading.

— [GROOM]
