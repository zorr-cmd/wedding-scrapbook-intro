A Letter·From Alex

Nepal.

Whether you’re reading this in 2026 or in 2036, I want you to know that I miss you. It’s been a while — give me a call and let’s catch up!

I also want you to know how nostalgic making this scrapbook made me. I hope when you go through this, you feel that same sense of awe reflecting on how amazing our lives are and how many special moments we’ve been blessed to share with each other.

I wanted to include the epic and meaningful memories, but I made sure to also include the random, simple selfies. Because honestly, that’s what the majority of life is. We don’t need grand adventures to enjoy our time together. Because with you, life and time just flows naturally. And that’s why I love you.

Life is long, and I know that there are countless more memories, grand and simple, for us to share together.

Love you with all my heart, brother
Alex :)
