A Poem·For Nepal's Wedding
In a Few Verses.
Eight stanzas and a postscript, by Anukul
I.
Hey my remarkable friend Nepal, I hope this poem finds you well. Can’t believe you are finally there Soon to be walking down the aisle as a pair.
II.
Went from late night tennis to big movie sets, You even made it down a few red carpets. I love that you chose to pursue your dream, Hopefully one day we’ll see your work on the big screen.
III.
It’s unbelievable to think how far you have come, Hard to imagine the days when we were dumb. Let’s take a quick look and reminisce the past And remember the moments that were a blast.
IV.
Driving down the highway for the beach joyrides, Cranking the music and watching the tides. Finding a place where we would stop and chill, Looking back, time felt as if it were still.
V.
More often than not we would play board games, Often we would play Catan or Codenames. Playing Catan with you was quite the delight But playing it out till the end would make the night.
