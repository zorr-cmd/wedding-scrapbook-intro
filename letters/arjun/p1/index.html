A Letter·June 6

Chapters.

Nepal,

It’s honestly crazy looking back at how long we’ve known each other. From getting close senior year of high school to somehow still being in each other’s lives all these years later through every SD BOYZ trip and questionable decision, you’ve been part of some of my favorite chapters.

Some of my earliest memories with you are the classic high school ones: hitting the gym, going on late-night drives, and convincing ourselves that Highland Valley Road was a perfectly normal place to test the limits of physics. Looking back, it’s probably a miracle we made it out of that era in one piece. But that was always the fun of being around you — even the simplest plan had a way of turning into a story.

Of course, I also have to give credit where it’s due: nobody has ever trusted the American System quite like you. Somehow, against all odds, it keeps working.
