A Letter·June 6

Nepal.

I am so grateful that life gave us the chance to grow closer over the years. You are the kind of friend who makes every room feel bigger and every moment worth showing up for. I have been endlessly lucky to call you my friend.

You have a rare gift: the ability to make the people around you feel truly seen. Your enthusiasm is contagious, your creativity is inspiring, and your belief in the people you love has meant more than you know.

Though this chapter brings a wonderful new beginning, nothing changes between us. I will always have your back, just as I know you will always have mine. The boys and I will always be in your corner, cheering the loudest.

— Dillan
