Nepal!

What the! Is this real life? I’m writing an entry in a scrapbook for my boy, who’s getting MARRIED?!

You know, I believe that when it’s all said and done, all we really have to our name is the friends and family we hold close to our hearts and all the memories we’ve made along the way. I feel lucky to count you within those friends and memories. I remember meeting you through Alex & Kris. An off-the-well-traveled-path kid with a dorky, goofy smile that I can never forget. You’ve grown in ways I can’t believe. Most striking to me, is that I feel like you’ve become comfortable embraced the person you are deep down. I see it in the way that you’re so full of life. In the way that you connect deeply and effortlessly with people. In the way that you take on the world with your movies, dreams, and ambitions. Really, it’s inspiring.

And that raw, authentic “you” that you put out into the world found a wonderful human (Daria) to receive that and reciprocate. I couldn’t be happier for you. I can’t wait to see the two of you together and celebrate you both, our friendship, and life.

I’m going to end with a photo of one of my favorite memories of us: SD Boys Reunion 2025. You moved mountains to make it work despite your crazy schedule, and that’s what I love about you.

I love you unconditionally,
Shubham
