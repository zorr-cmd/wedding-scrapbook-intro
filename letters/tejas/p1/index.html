Family.

Nepal,

I still remember when I first met you, finding out you lived in my neighborhood and thinking you had the coolest name I’d ever heard.

It’s been incredible watching the person you’ve become. You’re always willing to help others, eager to share what you love, and fearless when it comes to chasing new experiences. I’ve always admired your curiosity, open-mindedness, and the way you genuinely believe in the people around you. No one hypes up their friends quite like you do, and it makes us feel unstoppable.

You have a remarkable presence and a genuine love for the people and things that matter to you. Getting to know you over the years has been one of the great joys of my life, and I’m so excited to call you family.

Congratulations on this next chapter with Daria. I can’t wait to see all the adventures ahead, the films you’ll create, the students’ lives you will change, and the impact you’ll continue to have on everyone around you. Keep shooting for the stars while staying grounded in the values that make you who you are.

Wishing you both a lifetime of happiness, love, and unforgettable memories. And when Bear Boy finally hits the big screen, I’ll be right there with the SD boys and Daria celebrating louder than anyone.

— Tejas
