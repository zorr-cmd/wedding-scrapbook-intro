Across continents, across years, this is the visual record of a group of friends who refuse to grow up alone. From the Pyramids of Giza to the red rocks of Sedona, from the neon canyons of Times Square to the alpine glow of Mt. Shasta — they have been each other's witnesses. On the occasion of the groom's wedding, we offer eleven small windows into a brotherhood.
