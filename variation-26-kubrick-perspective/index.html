DIRECTOR'S  NOTE  ·  STANLEY  KUBRICK  [SIGNATURE STYLE]

"Symmetry is not perfection. Symmetry is permission to look. When the frame is balanced, the eye can rest. When the eye can rest, the audience can feel.

These eleven frames have been recomposed from their original casual photography into the visual grammar of Kubrick — perspective lines converging at the vanishing point, ensemble centered, color slightly drained to let the geometry breathe.

The director hopes you notice. The director hopes you don't."
