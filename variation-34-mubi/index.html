The Brothers

An ensemble film by [GROOM]

2017–2026  ·  11 Chapters  ·  Color  ·  English  ·  ★★★★★ 5/5
Synopsis

A decade of friendship rendered as a single film. [GROOM]'s The Brothers assembles eleven photographic moments — taken across four continents and ten years — into a quiet ensemble portrait. Less a documentary than a witness statement, the film tracks eight men through their twenties and into the wedding of one of them. The viewer is invited to notice not what happens, but what stays.

From the Curator
Selected as part of our Modern Ensemble program. This film pairs naturally with Boyhood (Linklater, 2014), The Trip (Winterbottom, 2010), and Past Lives (Song, 2023).
