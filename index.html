Film · Variation 07

Brothers. A Shot List. — Director's Storyboard Binder

A pre-production binder rendered on ruled storyboard paper with punched holes. Page 1: full shot list spreadsheet (SC#, SH#, EXT/INT, LENS, NOTES) and hero storyboard panels with scene slugs and camera direction. Page 2: remaining boards, plus inline-SVG lens diagram, 3-point lighting plan, and a wedding-day call sheet. Director's-red pencil for FINAL CUT marks.

Film · Variation 08

The Home Video Archive — VHS Tape Library

A 1990s VHS home video archive. Page 1: chyron title, transport controls (▶ PAUSE ⏪), shelf of Sharpie-labeled tape spines, and a CRT TV "now playing" with scan lines, burnt-in timestamp, and REC indicator. Page 2: tape index of all stills as mini-CRT frames with tracking errors and a closing chyron in Turkish (Şerefe / İyi yolculuklar).

Film · Variation 10

Project: Brothers — Director's Notebook

The most intimate variation. An open black Moleskine — ruled paper, binding shadow, dog-eared corner, coffee rings. Photos taped in as polaroid test shots with hand-scrawled lens notes, exposure logs, lens diagrams, blocking notes. Page 2 includes a yellow-legal-pad CALL SHEET with "NO BAD TAKES PERMITTED" and a postscript ending in Şerefe.
